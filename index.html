BIO

I am an Assistant Professor in the Digital Media Technologies Department at East Stroudsburg University in East Stroudsburg, PA. I specialize in Social Media, Digital Marketing, Website Design and Online Coding. I hold an M.F.A. from Cranbrook Academy of Art with a concentration in 2d Design and Painting and B.A. in filmmaking and painting from Montclair State University. I have held positions at a variety of digital advertising firms, including Digitas, where I was an Associate Creative Director, working on pharmaceutical advertising and websites for General Motors. I also held the position of Senior Production Artist at Organic Inc., working on multimedia production for Daimler Chrysler’s many automotive brands, and was Lead Designer at J. Walter Thompson, designing the Ford family of websites.

In addition to my current position at East Stroudsburg University, I have taught Website Production, Design, and Digital Art at Caldwell University, as well as lectured in the B.F.A Program at Montclair State University. I currently reside in West Orange, NJ.

New York

Set up by a computer, the quirky wonder of a detailed arrangement.

Tina Glengary / Creative Strategist

Tina has been living with her boyfriend Evan in a 1LDK
(1 Living Dining Kitchen) in Brooklyn for half a year.
They picked items that are highly designed and fresh.
They had trouble placing the furniture they had from before,
so the whole process of space and furniture placement
design was simulated by a computer.
As a result, a wonderful and orderly layout was completed.
Whether it is paintings, bear artwork, or a lot of figurines, it is perfectly cute, tasteful and original.

1. Living Room. The pink painting is by an artist friend, Nick D'Angelo.
2. Tina and her (love) dog Claire.
3. Chinese-made antique where small items are placed, and if you look closer
you will see that it's placed on its side.
4. From the music room, through the living room, the book shelves are in a row.
Bear artwork found on the net, created by a craft designer called Gaia.
5. Both of them like to collect children's toys,
and the collection is placed in a row.
